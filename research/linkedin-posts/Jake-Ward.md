Content:
85% of AI visibility has NOTHING to do with your site. It comes from what other people say about you.

In fact, only 13% comes from your own domain.

I keep saying earned beats owned for AI SEO, and here's the data to prove it:

Brands are 6.5x more likely to get cited through third-party sources than their own site. This is from an analysis of 21,311 brand mentions across ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity.

So if you're only optimising your own domain, you're ignoring 85% of where AI search actually finds you.

And nearly 90% of those third-party mentions come from listicles, comparisons, and reviews, i.e. the "best X tools" roundups you've been neglecting.

The playbook is clear:

1. Get mentioned in roundups and comparison content across trusted domains.

2. Track where you're showing up (and where you're not) across every AI platform with Mentions.

3. Stop treating your own site as the whole strategy.

Earned visibility isn't a nice-to-have anymore. It's 85% of the AI search game.
